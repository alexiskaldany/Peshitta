# Ezra 2

[[Ezr-01|Ezra - Chapter 1 ]] | [[Ezra]] | [[Ezr-03|Ezra - Chapter 3 ]]
***

###### v1
Now these were the people of the province who came from those captive exiles whom King Nebuchadnezzar of Babylon had carried captive to Babylonia; they returned to Jerusalem and Judah, all to their own towns.
###### v2
They came with Zerubbabel, Jeshua, Nehemiah, Seraiah, Reelaiah, Mordecai, Bilshan, Mispar, Bigvai, Rehum, and Baanah. The number of the Israelite people:
###### v3
the descendants of Parosh, two thousand one hundred seventy-two.
###### v4
Of Shephatiah, three hundred seventy-two.
###### v5
Of Arah, seven hundred seventy-five.
###### v6
Of Pahath-moab, namely the descendants of Jeshua and Joab, two thousand eight hundred twelve.
###### v7
Of Elam, one thousand two hundred fifty-four.
###### v8
Of Zattu, nine hundred forty-five.
###### v9
Of Zaccai, seven hundred sixty.
###### v10
Of Bani, six hundred forty-two.
###### v11
Of Bebai, six hundred twenty-three.
###### v12
Of Azgad, one thousand two hundred twenty-two.
###### v13
Of Adonikam, six hundred sixty-six.
###### v14
Of Bigvai, two thousand fifty-six.
###### v15
Of Adin, four hundred fifty-four.
###### v16
Of Ater, namely of Hezekiah, ninety-eight.
###### v17
Of Bezai, three hundred twenty-three.
###### v18
Of Jorah, one hundred twelve.
###### v19
Of Hashum, two hundred twenty-three.
###### v20
Of Gibbar, ninety-five.
###### v21
Of Bethlehem, one hundred twenty-three.
###### v22
The people of Netophah, fifty-six.
###### v23
Of Anathoth, one hundred twenty-eight.
###### v24
The descendants of Azmaveth, forty-two.
###### v25
Of Kiriatharim, Chephirah, and Beeroth, seven hundred forty-three.
###### v26
Of Ramah and Geba, six hundred twenty-one.
###### v27
The people of Michmas, one hundred twenty-two.
###### v28
Of Bethel and Ai, two hundred twenty-three.
###### v29
The descendants of Nebo, fifty-two.
###### v30
Of Magbish, one hundred fifty-six.
###### v31
Of the other Elam, one thousand two hundred fifty-four.
###### v32
Of Harim, three hundred twenty.
###### v33
Of Lod, Hadid, and Ono, seven hundred twenty-five.
###### v34
Of Jericho, three hundred forty-five.
###### v35
Of Senaah, three thousand six hundred thirty.
###### v36
The priests: the descendants of Jedaiah, of the house of Jeshua, nine hundred seventy-three.
###### v37
Of Immer, one thousand fifty-two.
###### v38
Of Pashhur, one thousand two hundred forty-seven.
###### v39
Of Harim, one thousand seventeen.
###### v40
The Levites: the descendants of Jeshua and Kadmiel, of the descendants of Hodaviah, seventy-four.
###### v41
The singers: the descendants of Asaph, one hundred twenty-eight.
###### v42
The descendants of the gatekeepers: of Shallum, of Ater, of Talmon, of Akkub, of Hatita, and of Shobai, in all one hundred thirty-nine.
###### v43
The temple servants: the descendants of Ziha, Hasupha, Tabbaoth,
###### v44
Keros, Siaha, Padon,
###### v45
Lebanah, Hagabah, Akkub,
###### v46
Hagab, Shamlai, Hanan,
###### v47
Giddel, Gahar, Reaiah,
###### v48
Rezin, Nekoda, Gazzam,
###### v49
Uzza, Paseah, Besai,
###### v50
Asnah, Meunim, Nephisim,
###### v51
Bakbuk, Hakupha, Harhur,
###### v52
Bazluth, Mehida, Harsha,
###### v53
Barkos, Sisera, Temah,
###### v54
Neziah, and Hatipha.
###### v55
The descendants of Solomon's servants: Sotai, Hassophereth, Peruda,
###### v56
Jaalah, Darkon, Giddel,
###### v57
Shephatiah, Hattil, Pochereth-hazzebaim, and Ami.
###### v58
All the temple servants and the descendants of Solomon's servants were three hundred ninety-two.
###### v59
The following were those who came up from Tel-melah, Tel-harsha, Cherub, Addan, and Immer, though they could not prove their families or their descent, whether they belonged to Israel:
###### v60
the descendants of Delaiah, Tobiah, and Nekoda, six hundred fifty-two.
###### v61
Also, of the descendants of the priests: the descendants of Habaiah, Hakkoz, and Barzillai (who had married one of the daughters of Barzillai the Gileadite, and was called by their name).
###### v62
These looked for their entries in the genealogical records, but they were not found there, and so they were excluded from the priesthood as unclean;
###### v63
the governor told them that they were not to partake of the most holy food, until there should be a priest to consult Urim and Thummim.
###### v64
The whole assembly together was forty-two thousand three hundred sixty,
###### v65
besides their male and female servants, of whom there were seven thousand three hundred thirty-seven; and they had two hundred male and female singers.
###### v66
They had seven hundred thirty-six horses, two hundred forty-five mules,
###### v67
four hundred thirty-five camels, and six thousand seven hundred twenty donkeys.
###### v68
As soon as they came to the house of the LORD in Jerusalem, some of the heads of families made freewill offerings for the house of God, to erect it on its site.
###### v69
According to their resources they gave to the building fund sixty-one thousand darics of gold, five thousand minas of silver, and one hundred priestly robes.
###### v70
The priests, the Levites, and some of the people lived in Jerusalem and its vicinity; and the singers, the gatekeepers, and the temple servants lived in their towns, and all Israel in their towns.
